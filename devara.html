DEVARA

TIMES NOW: DEVARA is a super hit film starring JR.NTR and the movie already collected 100cr share on it's first day. It is the first part of a two-part film. The film was officially announced in April 2021 under the tentative title NTR 30, as it is Rama Rao's 30th film in the leading role, while the official title was announced in May 2023. Principal photography commenced in April 2023 in Hyderabad followed with a schedule in Goa, which was again followed with a one in the former location. The film has music composed by Anirudh Ravichander, cinematography handled by R. Rathnavelu and editing by A. Sreekar Prasad.
